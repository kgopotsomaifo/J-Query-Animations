AFRICA RESTURANT

Our Story

The story of the AFRICA resturant is that it is a simple african resturant which enhances the continents flavors due to its unique simplicity

Our Food

Our food includes all the cuisines from all corners of the continent. We have Mahgerbi cuisine from the north, which is basically halal food. In the West, we have the famous jollof rice, in the South we have biltong and in the East, it is Nyama Choma(roasted meat).

Our People

The people of the AFRICA resturant are obviously the natives of the land, and they are proud of their culture and cuisine.
